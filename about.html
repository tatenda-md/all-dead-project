This website contains a nine-part serial horror story 'ALL DEAD' by Tatenda Mudavanhu. The story is set in Berlin. Each story is exactly 180 words and was originally written for Instagram reels.

The creator of the series, Tatenda Mudavanhu, is a Berlin-based writer, filmmaker and upcoming software engineer from Masvingo, Zimbabwe. When he is not writing, Tatenda uses his time to edit videos, tutor creative writing, listen to music, cook with the airfryer or bike up and down beautiful Berlin.
